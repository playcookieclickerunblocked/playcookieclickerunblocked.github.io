About Cookie Clicker

Cookie Clicker is an incremental game created by French programmer Julien "Orteil" Thiennot. The game has been in development since 2013. The goal of the game is to bake as many cookies as possible by clicking on a giant cookie and purchasing upgrades and buildings.

How to Play

  • Click the big cookie to earn cookies
  • Buy buildings to produce cookies automatically
  • Purchase upgrades to boost your production
  • Unlock achievements and discover secrets

Game Features

  • Hundreds of upgrades and achievements
  • Multiple minigames to discover
  • Regular updates with new content
  • Save your progress automatically
